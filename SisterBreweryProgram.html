Empowering Locals through Community-Centered Brewing

Donation Tiers

  • Community Supporter Tier

    Donation Range: $1,000 - $5,000

    Benefits:

    • Recognition as a Community Supporter on program materials and website.
    • Invitation to an exclusive virtual brewery tour and Q&A session with program organizers.
    • Access to educational resources and guides on community-centered brewing practices.

  • Brew Session Partner Tier

    Donation Range: $5,000 - $20,000

    Benefits:

    • All benefits from the Community Supporter tier.
    • Opportunity to host a virtual brew session with the sister brewery, led by experienced brewers from both breweries.
    • Customized recipe development session with the sister brewery, including access to unique or hard-to-find ingredients.
    • Established trade partnership with the sister brewery for ongoing collaboration and ingredient exchange.

  • Community Builder Tier

    Donation Range: $20,000 - $50,000

    Benefits:

    • All benefits from the Brew Session Partner tier.
    • In-person brew session at the sister brewery, including travel and accommodation expenses covered for up to two representatives from the donating brewery.
    • Exclusive access to quarterly workshops and seminars focused on community engagement, sustainability, and brewery management.
    • Priority consideration for future partnership opportunities and expansion initiatives within the program.

  • Visionary Partner Tier

    Donation Range: $50,000+

    Benefits:

    • All benefits from the Community Builder tier.
    • Recognition as a Visionary Partner in program communications and press releases.
    • Opportunity to co-host a community-centered brewing event with the sister brewery, featuring collaborative beer releases and local outreach initiatives.
    • Dedicated support from program staff for developing and implementing customized community engagement projects in the donating brewery's local area.

Additional Ideas

  • Collaborative Marketing Campaigns: Develop joint marketing materials and campaigns highlighting the partnership between the donating brewery and the sister brewery.
  • Mentorship Program: Pair breweries in your program with experienced mentors from the donating breweries.
  • Community Outreach Events: Organize joint community events and fundraisers with the donating brewery and the sister brewery.
