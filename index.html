About Us

Welcome to Brews Beyond Borders! We aim to empower entrepreneurs in emerging markets to start breweries!

Mission Statement: To empower local economies through sustainable entrepreneurship by establishing community-centered breweries. Our nonprofit will foster agricultural development by sourcing and roasting local supplies, enhance cultural understanding through exchange programs, and promote ecotourism by integrating American brewing expertise with local traditions and values.

Primary Service: Educational Services and Community Development

Principal Office Address: 6057 Routt RD, Hartsel, CO 80449

Courses

  • Legal Filing in the Brewing Industry: A detailed overview of the regulatory and legal framework critical to establishing and operating a brewery.
  • Design and Construction of Brewery Hardware: A comprehensive study of the technical aspects of brewery equipment and infrastructure.
  • Learning to Brew Beer: An in-depth exploration of the brewing process from grain to glass.
  • Fermentation Science: A focus on the biological and chemical processes behind fermentation.
  • Bottling and Kegging Techniques: Covers the final stages of the brewing process, focusing on preservation and packaging of beer.
  • Malting and Roasting: Training in selecting grains, controlling malting conditions, and roasting.
  • Ecotourism and Sustainable Brewing Practices: Explores the integration of brewery operations with local agriculture and tourism sectors.
  • Cultivation of Malts and Hops: Teaches the cultivation of barley and hops, focusing on sustainable farming techniques.
  • Supply Chain Management for Breweries: Examination of supply chain management tailored specifically for brewery operations.
  • Empowering Women in Brewery Entrepreneurship: Highlights the significant contributions women can make to the brewing industry.
